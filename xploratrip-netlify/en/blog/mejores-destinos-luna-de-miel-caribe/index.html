The 5 Best Caribbean Honeymoon Destinations from Texas (2026)

Romer Urbina · XploraTrip June 2026 Updated Jul 2, 2026 7 min read Plano, Texas

The Caribbean is, without question, the favorite honeymoon destination for Texas newlyweds. Turquoise waters, white sand, romantic resorts, and incredible food. But with so many islands and countries to choose from, how do you decide? In this guide we compare the 5 best Caribbean honeymoon destinations from Texas, with real prices for 2026.

Tip before you start: For a honeymoon, we always recommend booking ahead — at least 3 months in advance. Romantic packages (beach dinner, room decoration, spa) have limited availability, and the best resorts fill up fast.

How do you choose the right destination for you?

  • Tight budget: Cancún or Punta Cana. The best value in the Caribbean.
  • Guaranteed weather year-round: Aruba. No hurricane risk.
  • Cultural experience beyond the beach: Cancún (Mayan ruins, cenotes).
  • Vibe and atmosphere: Jamaica (reggae, food, one-of-a-kind atmosphere).
  • Maximum luxury regardless of price: St. Lucia. Simply spectacular.

Frequently asked questions

Which Caribbean destination is best for a honeymoon from Texas?

It depends on your budget and style. Punta Cana is the most popular option for value; Cancún/Riviera Maya is the closest from Texas; Jamaica is the most vibrant and romantic; Aruba has guaranteed sunshine year-round; St. Lucia is the most exclusive and luxurious. Get a quote with us and we'll help you choose based on your dates and budget.

How much does a Caribbean honeymoon from Texas cost?

A honeymoon package (flight + all-inclusive hotel for 7 nights) costs between $1,500 and $4,000 per person depending on the destination and resort. Punta Cana and Cancún are the most affordable; Aruba and St. Lucia are more exclusive. Booking 2–3 months ahead gets you the best rates.

When is the best time for a Caribbean honeymoon?

December through April is the ideal season across the Caribbean: temperatures of 25–30°C (77–86°F), no rain, and low humidity. Avoid August through October due to hurricane season. May and November are good in-between options with good weather and lower rates.

Do you need a passport to go to Punta Cana or Cancún from Texas?

Yes, all Caribbean destinations require a valid U.S. passport. Make sure your passport doesn't expire during the trip. If you're a permanent resident, check each country's specific requirements, since they vary.
