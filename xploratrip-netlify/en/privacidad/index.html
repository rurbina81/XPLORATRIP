Last updated: June 2026

Effective as of June 1, 2026

XploraTrip operates the website www.xploratrip.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our site, you accept the terms described here.

We collect information that you provide to us directly, including:

  • Full name and contact details (phone, email)

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process travel quotes and bookings

  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Our website may use the Meta (Facebook) Pixel, an analytics tool that helps us measure the effectiveness of our advertising. The Pixel may collect information about your visits to our site and the actions you take.

This information is shared with Meta Platforms Inc. and is used to:

  • Measure conversions from our Facebook and Instagram ads
  • Show you travel offers relevant to your interests on Facebook and Instagram
  • Build custom audiences for advertising

You can control Meta's use of your data at: facebook.com/privacy/policy and manage your ad preferences at facebook.com/ads/preferences.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on the site. The types of cookies we may use include:

You can configure your browser to reject cookies, though this may affect the site's functionality.

5. Sharing information with third parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share data with third parties in the following cases:

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our service and comply with legal obligations. Active client data is retained for the duration of the business relationship and up to 3 years after the last transaction.

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We recommend not sharing sensitive information (such as full passport numbers) through unsecured channels.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@xploratrip.com.
