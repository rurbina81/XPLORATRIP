Founder & CEO

Romer Urbina

Engineer · Entrepreneur · Founder of XploraTrip

I'm a Computer Engineer — and engineering has never really left me. It lives behind every project I take on: I use it to bring order to processes, automate what used to take hours, and build systems that turn travel from a headache into something simple. But even as a young man, there was something else: the drive to see the world. I started by helping family and friends plan their trips, barely noticing that was where my calling lived. Country after country, I discovered that travel has a code — and that whoever learns to read it can change the course of entire families' lives.

In 2015, I brought the two together. I didn't trade engineering for travel: I merged them. That's how XploraTrip was born — today with clients across Latin America, North America, and Europe, especially Spain.

I'm Venezuelan and I live in Texas. I understand exactly what it's like to live far from your family — and I've seen too many people watch the excitement of a trip turn into a nightmare, with one obstacle after another at every step. I've helped manage the return to Venezuela for many people, and when it was my own family's turn, I lived through every one of those walls myself. That's why I know exactly how to knock them down for you — and how simple everything becomes when you have someone beside you who already knows the way, and has the tools to clear it.

Our story

From travelers to travel advisors

It all started with a family that wanted to come home.

1
The beginning

A personal need becomes a mission

While helping our own family navigate the process of returning to Venezuela — with expired documents, confusing routes, and little clear information — we saw that thousands of Venezuelans were facing the same thing alone.

2
The learning

Specializing in what no one else masters

We studied every airline, every route, every type of document. We learned what works and what doesn't — and started sharing that knowledge with friends and people in the community.

3
2015 · The birth

Engineering + travel: XploraTrip is born

Romer merges his two worlds and formalizes XploraTrip. The promise from day one: direct attention, fair prices, and friction-free processes for the entire Venezuelan and Latino community in the USA.
