The income model

5 ways to generate income

One business, several income streams. Combine the ones that fit your style — selling travel, building community, or both.

1

Direct travel sales

Uncapped commissions

Every flight, hotel, cruise, or package booked on your platform earns you a commission. The more you sell, the more you earn — no limit.

2

Referral bonuses

$15 to $275 per affiliate

When someone starts their business referred by you, you get a bonus based on the plan they join, plus BV points that add up.

3

Residual income

$15/month per subscriber

Active subscribers in your community generate recurring income month after month — income that doesn't depend on your time.

4

BV points — everything adds up

585 BV = $50

Every dollar that moves through your business accumulates volume points (1$ = 1 BV) that convert into money: 585 BV = $50.

5

Career plan

Bonuses from $75 to $8,000+

12 ranks with growing bonuses, 15% to 40% royalties at higher levels, recognition, and reward trips.
